Frequently Asked Questions

The onboarding process involves completing necessary documentation, attending orientation sessions, and meeting your team. Access the New Hire page to access the document which will guide you on following the initial steps.
The intranet portal can be accessed through the browser after logging in with your credentials.
Technical support can be found through the IT request portal, by raising a ticket. You can also do the same by contacting the IT team via email or phone.
Time off requests can be submitted through the employee portal. Once submitted, it will be reviewed and approved by your manager.
If you face an issue, convey it to the individual. If it persists, address the same with your manager. The HR is always available to sort out conflicts as well.
To know more about your leaves, access the employee portal.
The company supports remote work. Employees can work remotely based on their role and department's policies. Make sure to discuss your preferences with your manager.
Personal information can be updated via the employee portal.
To submit a new project or task request, reach out to your manager. He/she/they will review and approve the request.
